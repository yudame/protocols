## Key Sources

### Primary Sources - Regulatory Documents

**US GENIUS Act (2025)**
- Official Bill Text: https://www.congress.gov/bill/119th-congress/senate-bill/1582
- Congressional Research Service Overview: https://www.congress.gov/crs-product/IN12553
- Signed into law July 2025, establishes first comprehensive federal stablecoin framework

**EU Markets in Crypto-Assets (MiCA) Regulation**
- ESMA Official Page: https://www.esma.europa.eu/esmas-activities/digital-finance-and-innovation/markets-crypto-assets-regulation-mica
- Regulation (EU) 2023/1114, effective in phases from June-December 2024
- Sets uniform EU market rules for crypto-assets

**Financial Stability Board (FSB) Recommendations**
- Final Report (July 2023): https://www.fsb.org/2023/07/high-level-recommendations-for-the-regulation-supervision-and-oversight-of-global-stablecoin-arrangements-final-report/
- Original Report (October 2020): https://www.fsb.org/2020/10/regulation-supervision-and-oversight-of-global-stablecoin-arrangements/
- 10 high-level recommendations for global stablecoin regulation
