# Episode 5: Sustaining Excellence

## Research Prompt

Research teacher and caregiver wellbeing in early childhood education, particularly in intensive low-ratio or one-on-one settings. Explore burnout, emotional labor, sustainability factors, and what support structures enable long-term effectiveness. Include research on isolation, professional development, and ecological models of educator wellbeing.
