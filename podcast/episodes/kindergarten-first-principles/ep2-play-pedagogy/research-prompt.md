# Episode 2: Play as Pedagogy

## Research Prompt

Research the role of play in early childhood development from a neuroscience and developmental psychology perspective. Explore what happens cognitively and neurologically during different types of play, why play may be more than enrichment, and what the research says about structured versus unstructured time for children ages 4-6.
