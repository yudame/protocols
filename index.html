Research Methodology

Every episode follows a rigorous research process:

  • Primary sources first: Peer-reviewed studies, meta-analyses, and official documents
  • Effect sizes matter: Practical significance over p-values
  • Causation vs correlation: Clear distinction in all claims
  • Uncertainty acknowledged: Contradictory findings and limitations reported
  • Conflicts disclosed: Funding sources and potential biases noted
